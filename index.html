About Iva

Iva is a modern AI companion designed to be a helpful, friendly, and intelligent assistant. Powered by the latest advancements in large language models, Iva can answer your questions, help you brainstorm ideas, and engage in meaningful conversation.

This project was brought to life by Mohd Shawez Khan, a passionate developer dedicated to exploring the intersection of technology and human interaction. Iva represents a step towards creating more intuitive and accessible AI for everyone.

Features

Conversational AI

Engage in natural, human-like conversations. Iva understands context and provides relevant, intelligent responses.

Powered by Gemini

Leverages Google's powerful Gemini model for fast, accurate, and up-to-date information and creativity.

Dual-Theme UI

Switch between a sleek dark mode and a clean light mode to suit your preference, with your choice saved for next time.
